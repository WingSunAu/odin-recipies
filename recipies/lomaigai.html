Lo Mai Gai

Lo mai gai is a classic Cantonese dish which translates to "sticky rice chicken". Legend has it that it was first created in the humble night markets of Guangzhou. Originally, street vendors used to steam this with a bowl, and then introduced lotus leaves as a more convenient to-go option. Not to be confused by joong/zongzi, which traditionally uses bamboo leaves and a variety of other fillings, lo mai gai is characterized by its use of glutinous rice and chicken steamed in lotus leaves.
